📋 FAQ

Is UniMarket free to use?

Yes! UniMarket is completely free for all enrolled students. We charge no listing fees or commissions.

Who can sell on UniMarket?

Only verified university students with a valid student email and ID can buy or sell on UniMarket.

How are transactions protected?

We recommend meeting in safe public areas on campus. For online payments, we use a secure payment gateway. Always verify before transferring money.
